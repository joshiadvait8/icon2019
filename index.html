About ICON 2K19

Creative Thinking for Event Conference

ICON’19 clicking onto its decade year promises to be better and bigger. Laying its first stone in the year 2007, ICON has only moved forward onto becoming one of the most-awaited tech-fest covering different technologies and bringing out the big picture. Along with empowering its participants with its technical events, it also has something in its bag for the fun ones. So this year ICON is back exploring not just any technology but the future of technology – “ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE and VIRTUAL REALITY”.
